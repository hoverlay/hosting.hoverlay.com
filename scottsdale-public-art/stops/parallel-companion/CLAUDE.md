#Goal
a quiz to be used in an onsite AR experience in Scottsdale AZ, at the site of the artpiece “Parallel Companion” by Arizona-based artist Kristine Kollasch. The artwork celebrates Arizona’s natural beauty, including ringtails (the official state mammal ), Cactus Wren (official state bird) and two-tailed Swallowtail (official stage butterfly). It also showcases its mountains, clouds, cacti, rainstorms, and ever-present sunshine.
